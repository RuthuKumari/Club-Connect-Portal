Our Clubs

We offer a diverse range of clubs at Shri Vishnu Engineering College for Women, categorizing to various interests. Here are some of the clubs:

Mathelets Club

The Mathelets Club fosters a passion for mathematics. It organizes various activities such as quiz competitions, problem-solving workshops, and seminars to develop mathematical skills and logic.

ISTE Club

The Indian Society for Technical Education (ISTE) Club aims to promote the development of technical education. The club organizes technical events, workshops, and seminars to enhance the knowledge and skills of its members.

Empathy Club

The Empathy Club encourages students to engage in social work, community outreach, and volunteerism. The club focuses on building empathy and helping others through various initiatives.

Psychology Club

The Psychology Club is dedicated to understanding the human mind. The club organizes discussions, workshops, and activities that delve into various psychological topics, helping students explore the fascinating field of psychology.

Sports Club

The Sports Club promotes physical fitness and well-being. It organizes sports events, tournaments, and training sessions for students interested in improving their athletic abilities and teamwork skills.

Music Club

The Music Club is a hub for all music enthusiasts. It provides a platform for students to explore various music genres, participate in performances, and collaborate in musical events to cultivate their talents.

Style and Slay

The Style and Slay Club promotes fashion, beauty, and personal styling. The club organizes fashion shows, styling workshops, and discussions on trends to help students express their unique style and personality.

Dance Club

The Dance Club offers a creative outlet for students passionate about dancing. The club organizes dance rehearsals, performances, and competitions in various dance forms to encourage artistic expression and teamwork.

Eco Club

The Eco Club is dedicated to environmental awareness and sustainability. The club organizes tree plantation drives, recycling campaigns, and workshops to educate and encourage students to be environmentally responsible.

Toastmasters Club

The Toastmasters Club focuses on developing public speaking and leadership skills. Through regular meetings, speeches, and leadership exercises, students build confidence and improve their communication abilities.
